About

Reinald Kim Amplayo is currently a Masters degree student of Library and Information Science in the department of the same name at Yonsei University. He received his Bachelors of Science degree in Information Technology from Ateneo de Davao University in 2013. His current research interests lie on the intersection of machine learning and natural language processing, with concentration to neural and probabilistic language models.

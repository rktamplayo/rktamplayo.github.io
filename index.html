About Me

I am a final-year PhD Student in the School of Informatics at University of Edinburgh, under the supervision of Mirella Lapata. I am also fortunate to be awarded a Google PhD Fellowship. I also spent my Summer 2020 as a Research Intern at Google Brain working with Peter J. Liu.

My research interests lie on the intersection of machine learning and natural language processing, mainly on using discrete and latent information in natural language, such as topics, sentiments, and syntax, to enable customization on text generation tasks such as text summarization.

I am on the job market!

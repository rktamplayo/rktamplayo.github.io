I am a PhD Student in the School of Informatics at University of Edinburgh, under the supervision of Mirella Lapata. I am also fortunate to be awarded a Google PhD Fellowship.

My research interests lie on the intersection of machine learning and natural language processing, mainly on using discrete and latent information in natural language, such as topics, sentiments, and syntax, to enable customization on generation tasks such as in text summarization.

About Me

I am a research scientist at Google DeepMind based in London. Prior to this, I was a PhD Student in the School of Informatics at University of Edinburgh, under the supervision of Mirella Lapata. I was fortunate to be awarded a Google PhD Fellowship. I also have worked with Seung-won Hwang at Yonsei University, Peter J. Liu in Google Brain, and Sang-Woo Lee at NAVER AI Lab.

My research interests lie on the intersection of machine learning and natural language processing, mainly on long-form text generation with large language models. I am interested in the use of intermediate steps usually in the form of content plans that help LLMs generate long texts that are well-structured and coherent.
